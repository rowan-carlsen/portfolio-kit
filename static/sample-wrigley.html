If you have to focus on one type of concessions in your park, which would you choose?

Things to consider: In baseball games, people often judge a stadium partly by the food it serves. While catering to local preferences can make fans feel at home, "traditional" ballpark food may be perceived as bland or outdated. As a franchise, you need to consider the ideal food culture you would want in your ballpark. You also need to consider the identity of your audience and cater to as many different types of people as possible.

Reflection: When you consider concessions choices, think about revenues that will be generated. Which choice will sell in higher quantities? Which choice can be sold for higher prices? If your answers differ, which question is more important?
